Ingredients:
 - 150g Spaghetti
 - 50g Tomato Sauce
 - 20g Parmesan Cheese
 - 1 Tablespoon Salt

Directions:
 1. Bring a pot full of water to a boil and add the salt.

 2. Cook the spaghetti in boiling water, until the spaghetti have the firmness you prefer.

 3. Use a noodle sieve to separate the spaghetti from the water.

 4. Bring the tomato souce to a boil and add the spaghetti.

 5. Stir well, serve on a plate and add the grated parmesan on top.
